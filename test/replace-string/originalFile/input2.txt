Alice, a curious girl, followed her cat Alice into the garden. Suddenly, another Alice appeared, claiming to be from a parallel universe. Perplexed, the original Alice and the alternate Alice decided to explore together. They encountered a rabbit who mistook them both for Alice. Amused, the two Alices played along, leading to a comical mix-up. Eventually, they found a way to return the alternate Alice home, ensuring that our Alice's adventure remained unique.
